## TikTok/Reels Scripts

### Hook-First Format

```
[HOOK - 0-3 sec]
"This $30 tool replaced my entire tech stack"

[BODY - 3-12 sec]
- Show the problem (cluttered desktop, multiple apps)
- Reveal the solution (clean interface, all-in-one)
- Quick demo of key feature
- Social proof flash ("500K users")

[CTA - 12-15 sec]
"Link in bio for 50% off"
+ Show offer text on screen
```

---

### Story Format

```
[HOOK - 0-3 sec]
"I almost quit my business last month"

[PROBLEM - 3-7 sec]
"I was working 80 hours a week, burning out, making $5K/month"

[TURNING POINT - 7-10 sec]
"Then I discovered [product] and everything changed"

[RESULT - 10-13 sec]
"Now I work 20 hours and make $25K/month"

[CTA - 13-15 sec]
"Comment 'HOW' and I'll send you my exact setup"
```

---

### Controversial Take

```
[HOOK - 0-3 sec]
"Unpopular opinion: Notion is overrated"

[AGITATE - 3-8 sec]
"Don't get me wrong, it's great for notes
But for actual project management?
Too flexible. Too many options. Too much setup."

[SOLUTION - 8-12 sec]
"That's why I switched to [product]
Opinionated workflows that just work
No 10-hour setup videos needed"

[CTA - 12-15 sec]
"Link in bio if you're ready for simple"
```

## Email Subject Lines

### Curiosity Gap
- "The $0 tool that replaced my $500/month stack"
- "Why I deleted [popular tool] (and what I use now)"
- "I was wrong about [topic]"

### Urgency/Scarcity
- "Final hours: 50% off ends at midnight"
- "[Name], your spot expires in 4 hours"
- "Only 23 seats left for tomorrow's workshop"

### Benefit-Driven
- "Get 2 hours back every day (here's how)"
- "The exact template that 10x'd our conversions"
- "Your step-by-step guide to [desired outcome]"

### Question Format
- "Still using spreadsheets for this?"
- "What if you could [benefit] without [pain]?"
- "Ready to finally [solve problem]?"

### Personal/Direct
- "[Name], quick question"
- "Can I share something with you?"
- "You're invited (seriously)"
