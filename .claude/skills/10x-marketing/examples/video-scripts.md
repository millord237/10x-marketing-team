# Video Script Examples

## Social Media Video (15 seconds)

### Product Reveal Script

```
TIMING: 15 seconds at 30fps (450 frames)

---

[HOOK: 0-3 seconds | Frames 0-90]

VISUAL: Quick zoom into phone screen showing cluttered apps
TEXT ON SCREEN: "Still using 10 different apps?"
AUDIO: Trending sound / attention-grabbing sfx

---

[BODY: 3-10 seconds | Frames 90-300]

VISUAL: Smooth transition to clean app interface
TEXT ON SCREEN: "There's a better way"

VISUAL: Quick feature showcase (3 features, 2 sec each)
- Feature 1: "All-in-one dashboard" + animation
- Feature 2: "AI-powered suggestions" + animation
- Feature 3: "One-click automation" + animation

AUDIO: Upbeat background music

---

[CTA: 10-15 seconds | Frames 300-450]

VISUAL: App logo animation with gradient background
TEXT ON SCREEN: "Try free for 14 days"
SECONDARY TEXT: "Link in bio"
AUDIO: Music fade out + click sfx

---

PROPS (Remotion):
{
  hook: "Still using 10 different apps?",
  bodyPoints: [
    "All-in-one dashboard",
    "AI-powered suggestions",
    "One-click automation"
  ],
  cta: "Link in bio",
  brandColor: "#0ea5e9",
  accentColor: "#d946ef"
}
```

## Product Demo Video (60 seconds)

### SaaS Feature Walkthrough

```
TIMING: 60 seconds at 30fps (1800 frames)

---

[INTRO: 0-6 seconds | Frames 0-180]

VISUAL: Animated logo entrance
TEXT: "[Product Name]"
SUBTEXT: "The future of [category]"
AUDIO: Brand sound / jingle

---

[FEATURE 1: 6-18 seconds | Frames 180-540]

TRANSITION: Smooth slide from left
VISUAL: Screen recording of Feature 1 in action
VOICEOVER/TEXT: "First, let's talk about [Feature 1]."

DEMO POINTS:
- Show the problem (2 sec)
- Show the solution (4 sec)
- Show the result (2 sec)

TEXT OVERLAY: "Save 2 hours daily"

---

[FEATURE 2: 18-30 seconds | Frames 540-900]

TRANSITION: Zoom transition
VISUAL: Screen recording of Feature 2
VOICEOVER/TEXT: "But that's just the beginning."

DEMO POINTS:
- Show unique capability
- Compare to old way (split screen)
- Reveal the impact

TEXT OVERLAY: "10x faster than [competitor]"

---

[FEATURE 3: 30-42 seconds | Frames 900-1260]

TRANSITION: Fade through white
VISUAL: Screen recording of Feature 3
VOICEOVER/TEXT: "And here's where it gets exciting."

DEMO POINTS:
- Show AI/automation in action
- Real results appearing
- Customer reaction (if available)

TEXT OVERLAY: "Powered by AI"

---

[SOCIAL PROOF: 42-50 seconds | Frames 1260-1500]

TRANSITION: Cards flying in
VISUAL: Customer logos scrolling
STATS:
- "10,000+ teams"
- "4.9/5 rating"

TEXT: "Ready to get started?"
CTA BUTTON: "Try Free for 14 Days"
SUBTEXT: "No credit card required"
LOGO: Fade in at end
AUDIO: Music crescendo + fade

---

PROPS (Remotion):
{
  productName: "[Product]",
  tagline: "The future of [category]",
  features: [
    {
      title: "Feature 1 Name",
      description: "Save 2 hours daily with automated workflows"

    {

      description: "AI-powered intelligence built in"
    }
  ],
  cta: "Try Free for 14 Days",
  brandColor: "#0ea5e9"
}
```

## Ad Creative Video (30 seconds)

### PAS Framework (Problem-Agitate-Solution)

```
TIMING: 30 seconds at 30fps (900 frames)

---

[PROBLEM: 0-8 seconds | Frames 0-240]

VISUAL: Dark, stressful imagery (overwhelmed person, cluttered desk)
TEXT (appearing word by word):
"Drowning in busywork?"
"Endless meetings?"
"No time for actual work?"

AUDIO: Tense, building music

---

[AGITATE: 8-16 seconds | Frames 240-480]

VISUAL: Stats appearing with impact
TEXT:
"The average professional wastes 28% of their day on admin tasks."
"That's 2.5 hours. Every. Single. Day."
"545 hours per year. Gone."

VISUAL: Clock animation showing time disappearing
AUDIO: Music intensifies

---

[SOLUTION: 16-24 seconds | Frames 480-720]

TRANSITION: Burst of light / relief
VISUAL: Clean, bright interface demo
TEXT: "What if you could get it all back?"

VISUAL: Quick product demo
- Task automated (1 sec)
- Report generated (1 sec)
- Calendar organized (1 sec)
- Time saved counter (2 sec)

TEXT: "Reclaim 10+ hours every week."
AUDIO: Uplifting, hopeful music

---

[CTA: 24-30 seconds | Frames 720-900]

VISUAL: Gradient background (brand colors)
TEXT: "Start your free trial"
URGENCY: "Limited time: 50% off first month"
CTA BUTTON: Pulsing "Get Started Free"
TRUST: "No credit card required • Cancel anytime"
LOGO: Fade in
AUDIO: Music resolves

---

PROPS (Remotion):
{
  problem: "Drowning in busywork?",
  solution: "Reclaim 10+ hours every week",
  socialProof: "Join 50,000+ professionals",
  cta: "Get Started Free",
  urgency: "50% off first month",
  brandColor: "#0ea5e9",
  accentColor: "#d946ef"
}
```

```

VISUAL: Product logo centered
TEXT: "Your transformation starts today."
CTA: "Try [Product] Free"
TRUST: "Join 10,000+ success stories"
AUDIO: Music fade out

---
```

## Script Writing Checklist

### Hook (First 3 Seconds)
- [ ] Stops the scroll immediately
- [ ] Creates curiosity or emotion
- [ ] Relates to target audience's pain
- [ ] No brand name yet (earn attention first)

### Body (Middle Section)
- [ ] One clear message per segment
- [ ] Show, don't just tell
- [ ] Include proof/social validation
- [ ] Build emotional momentum

### CTA (Final Seconds)
- [ ] Single, clear action
- [ ] Remove friction (free, no CC, etc.)
- [ ] Add urgency if genuine
- [ ] Include trust elements

### Technical
- [ ] Safe zones for platform UI
- [ ] Captions for sound-off viewing
- [ ] Readable text (3+ seconds visible)
- [ ] Brand colors consistent
- [ ] Logo appears (but not too early)
